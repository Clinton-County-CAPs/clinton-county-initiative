Clinton County, Missouri

About Us

The Clinton County Library District was established by the County Commissioners on October 10, 2024, per Section 182.015 of the Revised Statutes of Missouri. The Commissioners defined the district as all of the county except the city limits of Cameron, where a municipal library district already exists through a city tax levy.

Five residents — none of whom are elected officials — were appointed by the County Commissioners to the initial board to organize and raise the necessary resources to establish library services in the district. A tax levy supporting the district must be approved by the voters before services can be offered. A successful tax levy is also required before the district can pursue additional sources of private grants and other funding.

Trustees serve four-year terms except the first trustees. Two are appointed for one year, one for two years, one for three years, and one for four years from July 1, 2025, in addition to their first few months. Annually thereafter, before July 1, the County Commission will appoint successors.

The initial appointees represent a wide range of the county’s residents and include:

  • Christel Gollnick, President (Trimble, MO) - connect@clintoncountylibrary.org
  • Trey Talley, Vice President (Lathrop, MO) - ttalley@clintoncountylibrary.org
  • Jennifer Howerton, Secretary (Lathrop, MO) - jhowerton@clintoncountylibrary.org
  • Paul Pottier, Treasurer (Gower, MO) - ppottier@clintoncountylibrary.org
  • Tony Stone, Member at large (Plattsburg, MO) - tstone@clintoncountylibrary.org

The Clinton County Library Board of Trustees will oversee the library, hire a library director, set policy, and apportion funding through detailed annual budgeting and monthly monitoring processes.

Meetings

Meetings of the Clinton County Library Board of Trustees are generally held on the second Tuesday evening of each month at 6:00 p.m. at the Community Courtyard in the county seat of Plattsburg, MO, unless otherwise posted on this website. Meetings are open to the public.
